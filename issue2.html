Issue #2

Breaking News: Hilichurls Start Farming? Teapot Blueprint Discovery Shocks Fontaine!

Fontaine—where justice is served, water is everywhere, and apparently, Hilichurls are now in the agriculture business?

Earlier today, The Steambird uncovered a remarkable chest fiercely guarded by a group of Fontaine Hilichurls and a Mitachurl. Expecting riches, artifacts, or even contraband, our reporter braved the danger to reveal the shocking truth: Inside the chest was a teapot blueprint titled "Court of Justice Green Grocers."

That’s right. Hilichurls were protecting farming infrastructure.

What Could This Mean?

  • Hilichurls turning to agriculture? Could this be a peaceful movement? Are they tired of foraging and looting and ready to settle down into society?
  • A secret black-market produce operation? Is Fontaine's food industry about to face unexpected competition?
  • Court of Justice involvement? The name of the blueprint raises questions—who authorized this? Is the judicial system secretly supporting Hilichurlian self-sufficiency?

New Developments: The Gardenmek Conspiracy

Following this discovery, our investigation led us to an unusual sight: Gardemeks stationed near a Roger Airship, as well as a lone Roller Mek patrolling the hill above the shore. While their presence may seem routine, the proximity to Hilichurl activity raises questions. Who programmed them? What are they guarding?

Suspicious Treasure Hoarder Activity

A deeper dive led us to a hidden operation near the Court of Fontaine. Multiple Treasure Hoarders were spotted near small boats, one of which was actively being used. Among them, a higher-ranking member with a golden raven insignia was present. Upon their defeat, the leader mysteriously disappeared, leaving behind only a small wooden box with 1,000 Mora floating behind the boat.

The Invisible Ship Mystery

Hidden among shipwrecks, a ghostly blue ship flickered in and out of visibility, vanishing entirely upon close approach. Nearby, a strange floating device hovered above the water. What purpose does it serve? Who controls these spectral vessels?

Investigative Next Steps:

  • Track further Treasure Hoarder movements—was this a failed smuggling operation?
  • Study Gardemek behavior—are they really just on routine patrols?
  • Seek expert opinions on the ghostly ship—are we dealing with advanced technology, or something... paranormal?
